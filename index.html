About Mzolo's Commitment to Service and Community

About Mzolo's Services

Mzolo's Services is a dedicated partner based in Frankfort, committed to providing essential, reliable solutions for both residential and commercial needs. We specialize in services like on-time sand delivery, professional assistance during vehicle breakdowns, and meticulous post-construction clean-ups.

Our company is founded on the values of integrity and service, driven by the vision of Dr. M.I Mzolo. Dr. Mzolo personally oversees key operations, ensuring that the company's core commitment to excellence and community upliftment is maintained in every project.

What truly sets us apart is our commitment to the local community: Dr. Mzolo actively hires unemployed individuals from the area to assist with operations, ensuring that our projects not only meet customer expectations but also provide vital opportunities and make a positive local impact.

Our Comprehensive Services

Sand & Material Delivery

Prompt and precise delivery of various sand types and building materials for your construction, landscaping, or home renovation projects. We ensure the right load, right on schedule, with a focus on affordability.

Vehicle Recovery & Hauling

Expert assistance for stranded vehicles. Our tractor and specialized equipment ensure safe and efficient recovery from challenging terrains, getting you back on track quickly and affordably.

Site Clean-Up & Waste Management

Thorough clean-up services for construction debris, demolition rubble, and general waste. We leave your site immaculate, ensuring safety and compliance, at a price that fits your budget.

Tractor & Equipment Hire (with Operator)

Hire our robust tractor and other essential equipment, complete with a skilled operator, for land clearing, minor excavation, grading, and other heavy-duty tasks tailored to your needs.
