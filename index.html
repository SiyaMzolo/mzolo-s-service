About Mzolo's Commitment to Service and Community

                       

At Mzolo's Services, we are more than just a service provider; we are a dedicated partner committed to delivering essential solutions with efficiency and utmost reliability. Based in the heart of Frankfort, we cater to a diverse clientele, offering specialized services for both residential and commercial needs.

                       

Our foundation is built upon the vision and leadership of Dr. M.I Mzolo, the esteemed founder. Dr. Mzolo brings a wealth of experience and a deep commitment to community, stemming from his distinguished career as the former Principal of Tsebo Ulwazi and his profound spiritual guidance as the Pastor for the New Reformed Apostolic Church of South Africa, holding a PhD in Theology. Now a retired Principal, his values of integrity and service are at the core of our operations.

                       

What sets us apart is our dedication to the community: Dr. Mzolo, who personally oversees key operations including driving our powerful tractor and managing deliveries, actively hires unemployed individuals from the local area to assist with operations. This ensures that every project not only gets done right but also helps provide opportunities for local families.

                       

Whether it's ensuring on-time quality sand delivery right to your project site, offering prompt and professional assistance during vehicle breakdowns, or meticulously handling post-construction and demolition clean-ups, Mzolo's Services is your trusted expert. Our pride stems from an unwavering commitment to exceeding customer expectations and making a positive impact on the lives of those we work with.
